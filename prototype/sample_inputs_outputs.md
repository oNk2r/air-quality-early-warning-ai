# Sample Inputs and Outputs

## Example 1: Moderate Air Quality

**Input:**
- City: New Delhi
- AQI: 85
- Time of Day: Morning (8:00 AM)

**Output:**
AQI Category: Moderate

The air quality is currently in the moderate range. While generally acceptable for most people, sensitive individuals may experience minor respiratory discomfort.

Health Implications:
- Most people can engage in normal activities
- Sensitive groups (children, elderly, those with respiratory conditions) may experience mild symptoms

Recommended Actions:
- Sensitive individuals should consider reducing prolonged outdoor exertion
- General population can proceed with normal activities
- Monitor air quality updates throughout the day

Special Considerations:
- People with asthma or heart conditions should take extra precautions
- Consider indoor activities if symptoms develop

Outlook: Air quality may improve slightly by afternoon as wind patterns change, but expect conditions to remain in the moderate range.

---

## Example 2: Poor Air Quality

**Input:**
- City: Beijing
- AQI: 165
- Time of Day: Afternoon (2:00 PM)

**Output:**
AQI Category: Unhealthy

The air quality has reached unhealthy levels. All individuals should take precautions, with sensitive groups requiring extra care.

Health Implications:
- Everyone may begin to experience health effects
- Sensitive groups are likely to experience more serious effects
- Increased risk of respiratory irritation and cardiovascular stress

Recommended Actions:
- Reduce outdoor activities, especially prolonged exertion
- Sensitive groups should avoid outdoor activities
- Keep windows closed and use air purifiers if available
- Consider postponing non-essential outdoor activities

Special Considerations:
- Children, elderly, and those with pre-existing conditions should remain indoors
- If outdoor activity is necessary, use N95 masks
- Monitor symptoms and seek medical attention if severe

Outlook: Conditions are expected to persist through evening. Air quality may improve overnight, but check updates before planning tomorrow's activities.

## Example 3: Good Air Quality

**Input:**
- City: Vancouver
- AQI: 35
- Time of Day: Evening (6:00 PM)

**Output:**
AQI Category: Good

Air quality is satisfactory and poses little to no risk to public health.

Health Implications:
- No health concerns for the general population
- Safe for all outdoor activities

Recommended Actions:
- All outdoor activities are safe and recommended
- No special precautions needed
- Good time for outdoor exercise and recreation

Special Considerations: None required.

Outlook: Conditions are expected to remain favorable through the night and into tomorrow.
